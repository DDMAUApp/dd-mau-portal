Employee SMS Program

DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery · Last updated July 8, 2026

DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery (operating as Andrew & Vy Catering LLC in Webster Groves, Missouri and forsis LLC in Maryland Heights, Missouri, together "DD Mau," "we," "us") sends operational text messages to its own W-2 employees. This page describes that program. We do not send marketing SMS and we do not text restaurant customers.

Who receives messages

Only active DD Mau employees who have opted in. Recipients are hourly and salaried staff at our two Missouri locations. The general public cannot sign up for these messages.

How employees opt in (Call to Action)

Consent is collected during new-hire onboarding and in employee account settings, inside our employee portal at app.ddmaustl.com. The portal is an internal employment tool and requires an employee sign-in; the opt-in step presents the following disclosure, which the employee must affirmatively accept before any SMS is sent:

"By opting in, you agree to receive urgent operational text messages from DD Mau (shift reminders, coverage requests, schedule changes, weather closures, 86 alerts). Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel. Reply HELP for help."

An employee's opt-in status, the consent text version they agreed to, and the timestamp are stored in our records. Employees may also give or revoke consent by speaking with a manager, who records it in the same system with an audit entry.

What we send

  • Shift reminders (e.g., one hour before a scheduled shift)
  • Schedule changes and shift-coverage requests / approvals
  • Time-off request decisions
  • Urgent operational announcements (e.g., weather closure, out-of-stock "86" alerts)
  • Onboarding and app-setup reminders
  • Reminders to read an important internal message

Sample messages

DD Mau: Your shift starts at 4:00 PM at Webster. Reply STOP to opt out.
DD Mau: Hi Ana, please open the DD Mau app and turn on notifications so you don't miss schedule alerts. https://app.ddmaustl.com/ Reply STOP to opt out.

Frequency, rates, and opt-out

  • Message frequency varies with the employee's schedule and restaurant operations.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation and nothing further. Reply START to re-subscribe.
  • Reply HELP for help, or contact your manager.
  • Opting out of SMS does not affect employment; the same information remains available in the employee portal.

Privacy

Phone numbers and consent records are used only for this employee messaging program and are never sold or shared for marketing. See our Privacy Policy and Terms.
