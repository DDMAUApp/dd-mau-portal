Privacy Policy

DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery · Effective May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery (operating as Andrew & Vy Catering LLC in Webster Groves, Missouri and forsis LLC in Maryland Heights, Missouri, together "DD Mau," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information about our employees and job applicants when they use the DD Mau Staff Portal (the "Portal") at app.ddmaustl.com.

Who this policy applies to

Active W-2 employees, on-shift workers, and job applicants who have started an application or onboarding flow with DD Mau. The Portal is an internal employment tool. It is not a consumer product and is not used to communicate with restaurant customers.

What we collect

  • Identity and contact: legal name, mobile phone number, personal email, mailing address, date of birth, government ID and tax identifiers (Social Security number, W-4 / I-9 inputs), uploaded ID documents.
  • Employment: position, location, hire date, wage, schedule, time worked, training progress, performance notes captured by managers.
  • Operational: task check-offs, photos uploaded as proof of completion, sauce production logs, food safety logs, maintenance reports.
  • Communication: the content and timestamps of in-app chat messages between you and other DD Mau staff, and SMS messages sent to or from your mobile number through our messaging vendor.
  • Device / technical: browser type, IP address at sign-in, push notification tokens (only stored if you enable push), session timestamps and audit logs of sensitive actions.

How we use it

  • Run payroll, scheduling, and the day-to-day operation of the restaurants.
  • Send you operational SMS and push notifications you have consented to receive (shift reminders, sauce production requests, food-safety log reminders, equipment maintenance alerts, onboarding paperwork notifications, and other internal work communications).
  • Comply with federal, Missouri state, and local employment law (Form I-9, Form W-4, tax withholding, OSHA, wage and hour, health-department recordkeeping).
  • Investigate disputes, complaints, accidents, and policy violations.

Text messages (SMS)

By providing your mobile number on your employee record and signing the Employee Handbook Acknowledgment during onboarding, you consent to receive operational text messages from DD Mau through our SMS vendor, Twilio. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on operational need (typically zero to ten messages per workweek). Reply STOP to any DD Mau text message to opt out, or reply HELP for help. Opting out of SMS does not end your employment and does not stop other operational communications through the Portal, email, or in-person.

Who we share information with

We do not sell, rent, or share employee personal information for marketing purposes. Information is shared only with:

  • Service providers under contract bound to handle your data confidentially: Google Firebase (database, authentication, hosting), Twilio (SMS delivery), Toast (point-of-sale and labor data), and our payroll provider.
  • Government agencies as required by law (IRS, Social Security Administration, USCIS, state labor agencies, public health inspectors).
  • Legal counsel and insurers as necessary to defend or resolve claims.

How we protect information

Personal information is stored in Google Cloud's Firestore database with rules that restrict access to authenticated administrators and the employee themselves. ID documents and signed paperwork are stored in Firebase Storage behind short-lived access tokens. Action audit logs are retained so that sensitive accesses can be reviewed.

How long we keep it

We retain employment records for the duration of employment plus the period required by law (generally four years for tax records under IRS rules and three years for wage-hour records under the Fair Labor Standards Act). Audit logs are retained for at least one year. SMS delivery logs are retained for the period required by our SMS vendor and applicable law.

Your rights

  • You may review and update your personal information at any time by signing into the Portal or asking a manager.
  • You may opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP, as described above.
  • You may request a copy of the personal information we have about you by emailing the address below.
  • You may ask us to correct inaccurate information or delete information we no longer need to keep.

Children

The Portal is not directed to anyone under 14 years of age. Where Missouri minor work permits apply, the minor's parent or guardian receives the privacy disclosures alongside the work permit.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will post the updated policy at this URL with a new effective date and, when appropriate, notify employees through the Portal.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information may be directed to: DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery, Attn: Privacy Officer, Webster Groves, Missouri  ·  privacy@ddmaustl.com
