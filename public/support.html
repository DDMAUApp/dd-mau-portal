DD Mau Staff Portal · DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery

The DD Mau Staff Portal is the internal app used by employees of DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery (Webster Groves and Maryland Heights, Missouri) for scheduling, work chat, training, checklists, inventory, and onboarding paperwork. This page explains how to get help with the app.

Contact us

Email us and we'll respond within 1–2 business days:

andrew@ddmaustl.com

You can also ask any manager or owner at your restaurant in person.

Common questions

  • I can't sign in / I forgot my PIN. Your account is created by a manager. Ask a manager or owner to look up or reset your PIN in the Admin panel.
  • I'm a new hire and lost my onboarding link. Ask the manager who hired you to re-send your onboarding invite, or email us at the address above.
  • Push notifications aren't arriving. Make sure notifications are enabled for "DD Mau" in your phone's Settings, then re-open the app once so it can register.
  • Text messages (SMS). Reply HELP to any DD Mau text for help, or STOP to opt out. Opting out of texts does not affect your employment.
  • I want to delete my account. Open the in-app Sidebar and tap "Delete account," or email andrew@ddmaustl.com. See the account-deletion section of our Privacy Policy for details.

Who can use the app

The Portal is an internal employment tool for DD Mau staff and job applicants who have started an application or onboarding with DD Mau. Accounts are created by management; there is no public sign-up. It is not a consumer product and is not used to communicate with restaurant customers.
