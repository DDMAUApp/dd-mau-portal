# 🍜 Welcome to the DD Mau staff app

Your one-stop tool for everything you need to do your job at DD Mau Vietnamese Eatery — bilingual EN/ES, works on your phone, your iPad, or any computer. Sign in with your PIN and you're in.

> **Day 1 setup (do this first):**
> 1. Open the app in Safari or Chrome on your phone.
> 2. Tap **Share → Add to Home Screen** (iPhone) or **Install app** (Android/desktop). It now behaves like a native app — and only THEN do push notifications work on iPhone.
> 3. Allow notifications when the app asks.
> 4. Open **Schedule → ⋯ → Set my availability** and tell us when you can work.

## Home Screen

Your launchpad. Big tiles to every part of the app. Tiles with a small **green stripe on the left** are your primary tools — the things you'll use every day (Schedule, Chat, Operations, Recipes, Print). The badge number on a tile tells you how many things are waiting for your attention.

- **🖨 Print** opens the Print Center for date labels, signs, "DO NOT USE" tags — anything you need to stick on a container.
- **🔒 Log out** is in the top-right corner. Always log out on a shared device.

---

## Chat

Where the whole team talks. There's a channel for everyone, channels for FOH/BOH, plus private DMs and group chats you can create yourself.

What you can do:
- **Send** messages, photos, voice notes, videos
- **@-mention** someone to get their attention (they get a louder ping)
- **Polls** for quick team decisions ("who's bringing dessert Friday?")
- **Schedule a message** to send later
- **Reply** to a specific message (threads stay tidy)
- **Edit** your own message within 15 minutes
- **Translate** any message to your language with one tap (Spanish ↔ English)
- **Read receipts** — see who's read an important message
- **AI grammar fix** — tap the ✨ button before sending and your message gets a polish without changing your voice
- **Mute a channel** or set "mentions only" if it gets too loud — Settings → Notification settings

> When something is out of stock, tap the **🚫 86 button** inside the chat to alert everyone instantly.

## Schedule

See when you work + manage your time off + tell us your availability.

What you can do (staff):
- **View your week** — grid, day, or list mode
- **See holiday + food day chips** above the days (Independence Day, National Pho Day, etc.)
- **Swap a shift** with a coworker → manager approves
- **Request time off (PTO)** with reason + dates
- **Set your weekly availability** so managers know which days/hours you can work
- **Set your birthday** — get a shout-out from the team
- **Tap any day** to see who else is on/available
- **Print the week** as a clean one-page PDF
- **ICS export** — add your shifts to your phone's calendar app

Managers get a drag editor, target hours, auto-fill (fills open slots from availability), and a publish flow that notifies every affected staff member.

## Operations

The kitchen + ops home base. Sub-tabs at the top.

### Checklists (Food Safety Logs)
- Daily opening / closing / mid-shift checklists
- Tap to check off each item
- Auto-saves who completed what + when
- Health-inspector ready trail

### Inventory
- Count items by category, vendor, or in split mode
- Tap **🏷 Label** for a quick date-code sticker on a freshly-opened container (lettuce, brined chicken, batch of broth)
- Tap **🖨 Print** for any free-form sign you need
- **Cart**: items you've counted go here automatically. Compare vendor prices side-by-side, pick the cheapest, **place a real order in Order Mode** with vendor toggle and partial-fill tracking
- Search bar with AI ("anything for pho", "spicy", "vegan") finds what plain text search misses

### Prep List + Sauce Log
Day-to-day prep checklist + sauce production log.

### 86 (out of stock)
Items you're out of. Marks back in stock when you have it again.

## Recipes

Bilingual recipe book — every dish, every sauce, every prep.

What you can do:
- **Search** in English or Spanish ("wings", "alas", "spicy", "vegan")
- **Scale** quantities up or down for the batch size you need
- **Allergen filter**: pick an allergen and any recipe containing it gets a red 🚫 strip — useful when a guest has an allergy
- **🏷 Print a prep label** right from any recipe — item name + allergens + ingredients + prep date + use-by date + your name, all on a linerless sticker
- The page is geofence-protected — only works when you're at Webster or MD Heights

## Print Center

A Word-style mini print app for anything you need on a sticker.

What you can do:
- Type any text (multi-line)
- Pick size: **Small / Normal / Large / Huge**
- **Bold** + alignment (left / center / right)
- **One-tap templates**: 🚫 Broken / ⚠ Quarantine / 🚨 Allergen warning / 🔖 Reserved / 🗓 Date label
- **Auto-stamps**: add date+time and your signature with one toggle
- **Copies 1-20** — print a stack of identical labels in one go
- **Live preview** before you print
- Sees your **last 6 prints** for quick reuse

Prints to an Epson TM-L100 linerless thermal printer at each location.

How to get there: 🖨 tile on the home screen, OR Operations → Inventory toolbar.

## Notifications

You get push notifications on your phone for things that matter:
- Chat mentions
- Shift offers + swap approvals/denials
- PTO request status
- 1 hour before your shift starts
- 86 alerts (when an item runs out)
- Onboarding doc reviews (admins)

**Quiet hours** are honored — set them in Chat → Notification settings if you don't want pings between 10 pm and 6 am. Mentions + emergency alerts still go through.

**SMS opt-in** is available if you want text messages too — ask a manager. Push is the default.

## Owner / Admin tools

If you're an admin (owners + designated managers):

- **Admin Panel** (⚙️ tile)
  - **Staff list** — PINs, locations, side (FOH/BOH), minor status, hours target, availability. Search staff by name / role / email / phone right at the top.
  - **Bulk Tag** — fast tag-everyone-at-once for FOH/BOH/minor flags. Floating Done button at the top so you don't have to scroll past 50 staff cards.
  - **Required Tasks** — push a "set your availability" or "opt into SMS" prompt that staff see at next login.
  - **🏷 Label printers** — configure the Epson TM-L100 IP per location + test print + view recent prints.
  - **Push notifications diagnostic** — test push to yourself.
  - **Recipe audit** — who opened what recipe, when.
  - **Inventory audit** — every add / subtract logged.
  - **Order log** — every real vendor order placed.
  - **Maintenance requests dashboard**.
  - **System refresh** — force every device to reload (emergencies only).

- **Onboarding** (🌹 tile inside Admin)
  - New hires fill paperwork from a token-gated public link.
  - Upload custom fillable PDF templates (Direct Deposit, etc.).
  - Review submissions, mark approved/rejected, move-to-complete.
  - **Apply form** (public job applications).

## Tips for new hires

1. **Install the app to your home screen.** That's how you get push notifications on iPhone.
2. **Set your availability** in Schedule on Day 1 — the manager builds next week's schedule from this.
3. **Watch your training modules** before your first shift on a station.
4. **When in doubt, ask in Chat** — there's always someone who knows.
5. **🏷 Date-label everything you prep** — it's faster than the old masking-tape method and looks better for the health inspector.
6. **Translate freely** — the team is bilingual; nobody minds either language.
7. **Lock the app when you walk away** — tap the 🔒 in the top-right.
